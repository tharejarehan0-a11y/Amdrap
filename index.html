WHAT IS A HACK CLUB


Hack Club is a global community for high school students who like building things with code. It runs student-led coding clubs, funds projects, hosts hackathons, and gives real support like mentorship and resources so teens can learn by actually creating, not just memorizing theory.

In Simple Terms: It is school, but for people who prefer making cool stuff instead of cramming for exams


WHY JOIN THIS CLUB


Our Hack Club exists for one simple belief: Coding is not reserved for toppers and the people thinking to pursue something in the field of science

OUR BELIEVE

  • You do not need pror knowledge to start coding.
  • Even someone who knows nothing today can build real projects tomorrow
  • School marks do not define how good you can be at coding or problem-solving.
  • You do not need to choose the science stream to be a great programmer


Coding is not about memorizing formulas or scoring marks. It is about memorizing formulas or scoring marks. It is about curiosity, logic, creativity, and the courage to try. These things are not taught by textbooks. They grow when you build, fail, and try again.

Our Focus

  • Learning by making real projects
  • Helping beginners feel confident instead of scared
  • Building skills that matter in the real world, not just exams
  • Creating a space where anyone can belong, regardless of background or stream
